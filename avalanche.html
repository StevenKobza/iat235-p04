How To Increase Your Odds of Surviving an Avalanche

  • Plunge an ice axe into the undersurface. This may help you near the top of the slide.

  • Shout. Others may hear you.

  • Get rid of gear; sacks, skis, etc

If You Are Caught in an Avalanche

  • Keep one hand in front of your face and try to maintain air space.

  • Try to main space for chest expansion by taking and holding a deep breath

  • Try to avoid panic and conserve energy. Your companions are probably searching for you.
